Santorini, Greece

The precious gem of the Aegean Sea

Things to See:

  • Akrotiri- Ruins of a prehistoric village
  • Santorini caldera- Volcanic crater
  • Santo Wines- Winery with a beautiful patio view
  • Ancient Thera- Ancient mountaintop city with ruins

Places to Stay:

  • Andronis Boutique Hotel
  • Carpe Diem Santorini
  • Chromata Hotel
  • Aqua Luxury Suites
